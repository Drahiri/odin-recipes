Poppy seed cake

Ingredients

  • 200 g flour
  • 150 g powdered sugar
  • 250 ml egg whites
  • 250 ml dry poppy seed
  • 250 g margarine
  • 1 tea spoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Melt the margarine
  2. Put all dry ingredients into a mixing bowl
  3. Mix dry ingredients by hand
  4. Add warm, melted margarine to mixing bowl
  5. Mix until ingrediends mix properly
  6. Whisk egg whites in separate bowl
  7. Add whites to main ingredients while mixing
  8. Put batter into baking tin lined with baking paper
  9. Bake at 200°C for approximately 40 minutes
