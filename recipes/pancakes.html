Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 250 ml flour
  • 250 ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spoons oil
  • 1 spoon sugar
  • 2 tea spoons baking powder
  • 1/4 tea spoon salt
  • 30 g vanilla sugar
  • Margarine to sauter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into mixing bowl
  2. Add oil, sugar, baking powder, sal, vanilla suger
  3. Add milk
  4. Mix together until sugars dissolves
  5. Add flour
  6. Mix until you get uniform batter
  7. Dissolve some margarine on the pan
  8. Pour 2-3 ladle of batter onto pan
  9. Sauter on small fire under lid
  10. Flip over when top stop being fluid
  11. Wait until bottom will be get darker
  12. Take pancake out
